PhotoGrabber

Download photos from Facebook

Usage

$ ./pg.py 
Open Browser [y/n]: y
Created new window in existing browser session.
Enter Token: *** REDACTED
Download Location [/home/tam7t/photograbber]: 
Target: me

Options
-------
u: Download all albums uploaded by the targets. (Use with --target)
t: Download all photos with the target tagged. (Use with --target)
c: Download full comment data. (Use with --target)
a: Download full album, even if just 1 photo has the tagged target. (Use with --target)
Input Options (e.g. 'cau' or 'caut'):utca               
Retrieving me's album data...
Retrieving me's tagged photo data...
Downloading photos
Complete!

Arguments

$ ./pg.py --help
usage: pg.py [-h] [--token TOKEN]
             [--list-targets {me,friends,pages,following,all}]
             [--list-albums LIST_ALBUMS [LIST_ALBUMS ...]]
             [--target TARGET [TARGET ...]] [-u] [-t] [-c] [-a]
             [--album ALBUM [ALBUM ...]] [--dir DIR] [--debug {info,debug}]

Download Facebook photos.

optional arguments:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
  --token TOKEN         Specify the OAuth token used to authenticate with
                        Facebook.
  --list-targets {me,friends,pages,following,all}
                        Display names and object_id's of potential targets
  --list-albums LIST_ALBUMS [LIST_ALBUMS ...]
                        List the albums uploaded by a target. Separate the
                        object_id's of targets with spaces.
  --target TARGET [TARGET ...]
                        Download targets. Separate the object_id's of people
                        or pages with spaces.
  -u                    Download all albums uploaded by the targets. (Use with
                        --target)
  -t                    Download all photos with the target tagged. (Use with
                        --target)
  -c                    Download full comment data. (Use with --target)
  -a                    Download full album, even if just 1 photo has the
                        tagged target. (Use with --target)
  --album ALBUM [ALBUM ...]
                        Download full albums. Separate the object_id's of the
                        albums with spaces.
  --dir DIR             Specify the directory to store the downloaded
                        information. (Use with --target or --album)
  --debug {info,debug}  Log extra debug information to pg.log

Authors and Contributors

PhotoGrabber is created and maintained by Tommy Murphy (@tam7t). The original idea for PhotoGrabber came from a script by @seancolyer. The first generation version of PhotoGrabber included a major contribution by [Bryce Boe] that added capability to download full albums and photo metadata including comments.
